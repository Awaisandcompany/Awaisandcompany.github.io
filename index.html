Our services extend to collaborating with regional revenue authorities, ensuring holistic and effective solutions for our clients.

Income Tax

Income tax is a direct tax imposed by the federal government on individuals and businesses based on their income, profits, and earnings.

Sales Tax

Sales tax is a consumption tax levied on the sale of goods and services, collected by both federal and provincial authorities in Pakistan.

Trademark

A trademark in Pakistan is a legally registered symbol, logo, or name that uniquely identifies a product or service, providing exclusive rights to its owner for its use and protection against unauthorized use by others.

Copyright

Copyright in Pakistan grants the creator of an original work exclusive rights to reproduce, distribute, and display their work, protecting literary, artistic, and intellectual creations for a specified period.

Firm Registration

Firm registration is the legal process through which businesses, particularly partnerships and sole proprietorships, are registered with the relevant authorities for taxation and regulatory purposes.

Company Registration

Company registration is the formal process of establishing a legal entity, such as a limited liability company (LLC) or corporation, under the Companies Act in Pakistan. This process provides a distinct legal identity to the business, separate from its owners.

Punjab Revenue Authority

The Punjab Revenue Authority (PRA) is a provincial tax collection agency in Pakistan's Punjab province responsible for collecting and administering sales tax on services.

KPK Revenue Authority

The Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Revenue Authority (KPRA) is the tax collection authority in Pakistan's Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, primarily responsible for collecting sales tax on services.

Balochistan Revenue Authority

The Balochistan Revenue Authority (BRA) is the tax collection agency in Pakistan's Balochistan province, primarily responsible for collecting sales tax on services.

Sindh Revenue Board

The Sindh Revenue Board (SRB) is a provincial tax collection agency in Pakistan's Sindh province, tasked with the collection of sales tax on services.
